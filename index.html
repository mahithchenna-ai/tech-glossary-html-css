Tech Glossary

Learn key terms in technology — simplified and beautifully explained.

Artificial Intelligence (AI)

The simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems.

Cybersecurity

Practices and technologies designed to protect systems, networks, and data from attacks or unauthorized access.

Robotics

The branch of technology dealing with the design, construction, and use of robots for automation.

Machine Learning

A subset of AI where systems learn and improve automatically from experience without explicit programming.
